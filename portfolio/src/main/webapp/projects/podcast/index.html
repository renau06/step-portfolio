Welcome to Take Home Pediatrics:
The Podcast

Take Home Pediatric is a weekly podcast that delivers useful information to parents about child health and development. It was created by Dr. Bhumi Upadhyay, a board certified pediatrician in Saint Petersburg, Florida. The mission of this podcast is to provide expert information and guidance supplementing your pediatric visits. We will. Have various expert guests to enlighten us on current pediatric topics.
